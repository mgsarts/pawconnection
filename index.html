Connecting Caring Humans

Our purpose is to connect those looking to help with organizations needing help. Whether you are looking to volunteer your time at a shelter, donate items or money or help transport animals to find a home, we will help you find the best way to use your time and talent.

We work in the background to assist animal shelters, rescues and community organizations in ways they request. Our primary role is coordinating transport of animals between shelters, rescues and forever homes. Our assistance has also been requested in using our transportation network to distribute donated goods.

As an animal advocacy group, we also work to be a source of information for the public. This comes from our continuously growing knowledge bank that you can search and our dedication to helping you find the information if we don't already have it.

If you would like to join our transportation network, please click the button below and fill out the form. Someone from our office will be in contact with you. We appreciate you.

Connecting Your Community

We are always looking to connect new communities to our network. Maybe you run a rescue or volunteer at animal shelter or are an individual looking for ways to help, where can you start?

Contact us. It is that simple. We are a small office and would love to hear from you. Send us a note about who you are, if you have specific needs, are you wanting to volunteer, anything to get the conversation.

If your community does not have a Trap, Neuter, Return (TNR) program in place and you would like guidance in navigating the channels to set one up, we urge you contact us. This is one of the most effective ways to control feline overpopulation.

At the end of the day, we all have ways we can help. Do not hesitate to start the conversation so you can make a real difference for animals in need.

Thank you.
